About Us

Professional lawn care and maintenance dedicated to meeting customer expectations and beyond. At Legacy Lawn Masters, we take pride in every blade of grass. Whether it's weekly mowing, seasonal cleanups, or complete landscaping transformations, our team is committed to delivering beautiful results backed by dependable service.

Legacy Lawn Masters is locally owned and operated by Jack Fleer-Willsie, a proud Lutz native and current Master’s student at the University of South Florida. Jack grew up surrounded by the green industry — with his family providing landscaping services to the Lutz community for over 25 years. Building on that legacy, he founded Legacy Lawn Masters to continue delivering the same trusted care, quality, and attention to detail that local homeowners have come to rely on for decades.
